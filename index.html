I am a Petroleum and Mining Engineering graduate from SUST (Shahjalal University of Science and Technology). I have a strong background in engineering and have gained valuable experience working with different organizations during my time at university, which has improved my communication and networking skills.

I enjoy working with data analysis, GIS, and remote sensing. I love uncovering insights from data and ensuring that workplaces are safe and efficient. I'm always eager to learn new things and take on challenges that help me grow.
